# Privacy Policy

Effective date: [DATE]

Bu mu, O mu? is a casual entertainment app that lets users answer short two-option questions and receive a fun result profile.

## Data Collection

The app does not require account creation.

The app does not collect personal information such as name, email address, phone number, precise location, contacts, photos, microphone data, camera data, or payment information.

Quiz answers are processed locally on the device to generate an entertainment result profile.

## Data Sharing

The app does not sell user data.

The app does not share personal user data with third parties.

When the user chooses to share a result, the app opens the device's native share sheet. The user controls which app or service receives the shared text.

## Analytics and Advertising

The app does not currently use analytics SDKs.

The app does not currently show ads.

The app does not include in-app purchases or subscriptions.

## Children

The app is intended for a general audience and is not specifically directed to children under 13.

## Security

The app is designed to work without requiring personal accounts or sensitive user data.

## Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be reflected by changing the effective date above.
