# Content Rating Notes

## Purpose

This document records the recommended Play Console content rating answers for the first release of Bu mu, O mu?

The app is a casual two-option preference quiz with entertainment-only result profiles.

## Expected app content

- Violence: No
- Sexual content: No
- Profanity: No
- Controlled substances: No
- Gambling: No
- User-generated content: No
- Location sharing: No
- Digital purchases: No
- Online interaction between users: No
- User account system: No
- Ads: No

## Expected rating

The expected result is a low/general audience rating, such as Everyone or equivalent.

Final rating is determined by the Play Console questionnaire.

## Reviewer-facing access note

No login required.

Open the app and tap the start button to begin the quiz.

## Important positioning note

This app is not a scientific personality test.

The result profiles are for entertainment purposes only.

## Policy-sensitive claims to avoid

Avoid claiming:

- medical or psychological accuracy
- scientific personality analysis
- diagnosis or profiling
- financial, health, or life advice
- real compatibility scoring
